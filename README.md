# Aaytham Consulting (AyC) Overview

Aaytham Consulting is a Chennai-based partnership (founded 2023) positioning itself as a boutique management consultancy focused on growth and problem-solving. Its LinkedIn profile (3,322 followers as of 30-01-2026) emphasizes **“Accelerate Growth, Solve Problems, Unlock Potential”** and **“Data-Driven Business Consulting & Strategic Solutions”**.  
LinkedIn: https://in.linkedin.com/company/aaytham-consulting  

AYC expertise across three broad areas:

- **Business Consulting**: strategic planning, operational efficiency, financial advisory, organizational restructuring, change management  
- **Branding & Growth**: brand identity/positioning, content strategy, go-to-market and digital marketing, customer acquisition/retention, digital ecosystem development  
- **Social Impact Consulting**: community engagement, CSR/PPPs, impact assessment, sustainability  

Website (Services): https://aaythamconsulting.com/

The website states:  
> “We excel in Business Strategy, Branding and Social Impact Consulting to drive your success and deliver impactful results.”  

Listing **personal branding** among its specialties, reflecting its branding/advertising heritage and a strong future vision.

Industries mentioned include healthcare, education, hospitality, manufacturing, lifestyle, e-commerce, law, and international trade.  
About Us: https://aaythamconsulting.com/about-us/

Overall, AyC presents itself as a full-spectrum consultant blending business strategy, branding, and social value to help mid-sized businesses grow sustainably.
