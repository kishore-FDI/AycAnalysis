Each cell above synthesizes public claims and analysis. Redseer emphasizes consumer/tech strategy and IPO guidance (https://redseer.com/about-us/). Aaytham is a Chennai-based boutique founded in 2023 focused on strategy, branding, and social impact (https://aaythamconsulting.com/).

## Summary

Aaytham differentiates through combined business strategy, brand-building, and social-impact consulting. Redseer and Avalon lead on strategic depth and scale. Vector excels in execution-heavy operational transformation. Praxis leverages AI and analytics for growth strategy. Indus Insights specializes in financial-services data science.
